Elden Square

In the beginning, where there was nothing at all, the world was shrouded by fog. But then came fire, and with fire came disparity, heath and cold life and death, and of course, light and dark. And from the dark, they came, the 4 kings of the darkness. They shattered the primordial soul and shared each part equally. Thus began the age of fire. But soon, the flame will fade, drowning this world in darkness. One of the kings has to sacrifice himself to revive the flame, but they all refused to do so. You, the chosen undead, have to show that you are worthy to revive the flame by killing all 4 kings.

Elden square is our latest and biggest project to date, it is set up in a massive open world map. In the unstable country of Lauthrek you can explore the world to research for the secrets scattered all over the world, or go directly for the head of the kings. We wanted to keep our philosophy of constant challenge, so the game may feel to rough for newcomers, but don't worry, you can seek help at any time by calling a friend of yours to explore and conquere this massive world.

Coming by the end of 2023, the game will be available on all new gen consoles (Playstation 5, Xbox Series) as well as PC. 3 render resolutions will be available for consoles :
Fidelity mode : "Tuned for the highest visual quality and targets 30 image per second"
Performance mode : "Tuned for the most responsive gameplau experience and targets 60 image per second"
Performance RayTracing mode : "Hybrid beetween fidelity and Performance mode with an upscaling at 4K"
