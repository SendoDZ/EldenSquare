Presentation of the From Hardware company

Known for creating one of the most influential series of video games ever released with le Light Souls games, From Hardware is known for creating rather hard but rewarding game experience. With the genius of our main game designer Icham Benabbas, we were able to create a coherent univers during our trip with the Light Souls franchise

As you may have guessed, challenge is one our main objective with our video games because we think that the feeling of surpassing the obstacle put in front of you is the greatest sensation that video games can give you, following this logic we wanted some fresh air but keep the challenge aspect, that's why we moved to a more free experience with Sekijo, inspired of many ninja games from our childhood like Tenshu.
